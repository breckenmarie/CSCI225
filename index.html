Introduction

Hi there! My name is Brecken Merrill. I'm a senior at the University of South Carolina Aiken, majoring in Computer Science with an applied concentration in Cyber Security. With this degree, I plan on getting a job in cybersecurity; however, if I get the opportunity, I would love to continue school and get my master's degree and potentially a Ph.D. in Computer Science. In addition to getting my Bachelor's Degree, I graduated from Central Carolina Technical College in 2022 with my Associate's Degree in Art.

Furthermore, I am the Vice President of USCA's Esports club. I am active on the school's Discord server and the Overwatch 2 team. I love playing video games, with Overwatch 2 being one of my favorite FPS games; I have been playing Overwatch 2 since 2018 (back when it was Overwatch 1)! I have recently gotten back into Fortnite as well. Some of my favorite single-player games include Red Dead Redemption 2 and Skyrim.

In addition to video games, I love sewing, drawing, and writing--pretty much anything creative! I also love to cosplay; I currently only have one cosplay, Kiriko from Overwatch 2, but I would love to expand my collection. My goal is to make my cosplays and props one day. I am also learning how to animate and hope to be able to make animations of my original characters one day.
